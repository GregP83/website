Fluidigm

Developed one of the company's most complex systems as main software developer. Automation machines consisted of:
- 9 motors (8 servo from Altera and one rotary,
- 2 cameras,
- pneumatic system (analog IO)
- temperature controllers (analog IO)
- around 20 binary I/O

Machine was used to align two elastic disks with a precision of 10um in below 5 min on average - time mainly used for heating or cooling (expanding or shrinking) of the elastic disk.
Implemented in C++ with MFC (Microsoft Foundation Class) and Cognex machine vision library.
