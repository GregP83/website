Audit service on Ardia Platform


Another crucial component of the Ardia platform is the Audit service, which is responsible for logging all user actions, configuration changes, and interactions with data and instruments. This service ensures comprehensive tracking of activities within the system. For example, any updates to user privileges, connections of new client PCs, modifications to instrument configurations, and actions performed on data such as acquisition, modification, or export, are logged and stored in the database. Users can access the log entries and utilize query and filtering capabilities to easily find specific information they are searching for. The Audit service enhances transparency, accountability, and traceability within the Ardia platform, providing valuable insights into user activities and system changes.
